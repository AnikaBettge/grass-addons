DESCRIPTION

i.landsat.acca is to implement the Automated Cloud-Cover Assessment (ACCA) Algorithm from Irish (2000) with the constant values for pass filter one from Irish et al. (2006). To do, it need the band numbers 2, 3, 4, 5, and 6 (band 61 of Landsat-7 ETM+).

The ACCA gives good results over most of the planet with the exception of ice sheets because ACCA operates on the premise that clouds are colder than the land surface they cover. The algorithm was designed to Landsat-7 ETM+ but because of they use reflectances it usefull also for Landsat-4/5 TM.

NOTES

i.landsat.acca use full raster and it do not take account the current region.

EXAMPLES

To make standard ACCA algorithm with two pass processing (flag 2) and filling cloud holes (flag f).

Reflectances in band rasters 226_62.toar.1, 226_62.toar.2 [...] and thermal band 226_62.toar.61 to output file 226_62.acca: 
i.landsat.acca -f2 band=226_62.toar out=226_62.acca
