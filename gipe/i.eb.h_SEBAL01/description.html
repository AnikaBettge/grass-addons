NAME

i.eb.h_SEBAL01 - computation of sensible heat flux [W/m2] after Bastiaanssen, 1995 in [1], used in this form in 2001 by [2].

DESCRIPTION

i.eb.h_SEBAL01 will calculate the sensible heat flux map (h0), given both maps of Net Radiation and soil Heat flux (Rn, g0) at instantaneous time, the surface roughness (z0m), a map of the altitude corrected temperature (t0dem), a point data of the frictional velocity (u*), a value of actual vapour pressure (ea[KPa]) and the (x,y) pairs for wet and dry pixels.

For more details on the algorithms see [1].

OPTIONS

NOTES

- z0m can be alculated by i.eb.z0m or i.eb.z0m0. - ea can be calculated with standard meteorological data. eoTmin=0.6108*EXP(17.27*Tmin/(Tmin+237.3)) eoTmax=0.6108*EXP(17.27*Tmax/(Tmax+237.3)) ea=(RH/100)/((eoTmin+eoTmax)/2) - t0dem = surface temperature + (altitude * 0.627 / 100)
