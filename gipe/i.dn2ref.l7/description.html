DESCRIPTION

i.dn2ref.l7 calculates the Top Of Atmosphere (TOA) reflectance for Landsat 7 visible and NIR bands (1,2,3,4,5,7) from DN values. It is useful after importing your Landsat 7 imagery from storage format that is generally in standard DN values range.
