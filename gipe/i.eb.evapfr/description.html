DESCRIPTION

r.eb.evapfr calculates the evaporative fraction after bastiaanssen (1995). It takes input of Net Radiation (see r.sun,r.eb.netrad), soil heat flux (see r.eb.g0) and sensible heat flux (see r.eb.h0). A flag adds a root zone empirical soil moisture output from the article of Makin, Molden and Bastiaanssen (2001).
