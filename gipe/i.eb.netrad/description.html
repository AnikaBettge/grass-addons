DESCRIPTION

i.eb.netrad calculates the net radiation at the time of satellite overpass, the way it is in the SEBAL model of bastiaanssen (1995). It takes input of Albedo, NDVI, Surface Skin temperature, time of satellite overpass, emissivity, difference of temperature from surface skin and about 2 m height, single-way transmissivity, Day of Year (DOY), and sun zenith angle.
