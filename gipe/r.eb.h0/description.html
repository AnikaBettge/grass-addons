DESCRIPTION

r.eb.h0 calculates the sensible heat flux approximation (h0), a flag allows the use of an affine transform from surface temperature after bastiaanssen (1995). It takes input of air density, air specific heat, difference of temperature between surface skin and a height of about 2m above, and the aerodynamic resistance to heat transport.
