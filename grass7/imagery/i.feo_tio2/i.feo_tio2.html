DESCRIPTION

i.feo_tio2 calculates the FeO or TiO2 contents. wt%TiO2 = 3.708 arctan ((R415/R750)-y0Ti)/(R750-s0Ti) (1) wt%FeO = -137.97 ( R750 sin(theta) + (R950/R750) cos(theta) ) + 57.46 (2) with theta = 1.3885 rad, the ‘average slope of the trends in the mare’ from Wilcox et al (2005).

NOTES

Initially created for Clementine data.

REFERENCES

(1) Lucey, P.G., Blewett, D.T., Jolliff, B.L., 2000. Lunar iron and titanium abundance algorithms based on final processing of Clementine ultraviolet-visible images. J. Geophys. Res. 105(E8): 20297-20305. (2) Wilcox, B.B., Lucey, P.G., Gillis, J.J., 2005. Mapping iron in the lunar mare: An improved approach. J. Geophys. Res. 110(E11):2156-2202.
