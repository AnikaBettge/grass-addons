NOME

r.green.biomassfor.technical.py - Estimates the quantity of woody biomass obtained from a forest surface where extraction is possible given a particular level of mechanisation

PAROLE CHIAVE

SINOPSI

r.green.biomassfor.technical.py
r.green.biomassfor.technical.py --help
r.green.biomassfor.technical.py [-r] dtm=name forest=name yield1=name yield_surface=name management=name treatment=name forest_roads=name output_prefix=name [roughness=name] [rivers=name] [lakes=name] slp_min_cc=float [slp_max_cc=float] [dist_max_cc=float] [slp_max_fw=float] [dist_max_fw=float] [slp_max_cop=float] [dist_max_cop=float] energy_tops_hf=float energy_cormometric_vol_hf=float energy_tops_cop=float [--overwrite] [--help] [--verbose] [--quiet] [--ui]

Flag:

-r
Remove all operational maps
--overwrite
Permetti al file di output di sovrascrivere file esistenti
--help
Print usage summary
--verbose
Output verboso del modulo
--quiet
Output quieto del modulo
--ui
Force launching GUI dialog

Parametri:

dtm=name [required]
Name of Digital terrain model map
forest=name [required]
Name of vector parcel map
Name of vector parcel map
yield1=name [required]
Map of forest yield (cubic meters)
yield_surface=name [required]
Map of stand surface (ha)
management=name [required]
Map of forest management (1: high forest, 2:coppice)
treatment=name [required]
Map of forest treatment (1: final felling, 2:thinning)
forest_roads=name [required]
Raster map of forest roads (0, 1)
output_prefix=name [required]
Prefix for technical bioenergy (HF,CC and total)
roughness=name
Name of roughness map
rivers=name
Raster map of rivers (0, 1)
lakes=name
Raster map of lakes (0, 1)
slp_min_cc=float [required]
Percent slope lower limit with Cable Crane
Default: 30.
slp_max_cc=float
Percent slope higher limit with Cable Crane
Default: 100.
dist_max_cc=float
Maximum distance with Cable Crane
Default: 800.
slp_max_fw=float
Percent slope higher limit with Forwarder
Default: 30.
dist_max_fw=float
Maximum distance with Forwarder
Default: 600.
slp_max_cop=float
Percent slope higher limit with other techniques for Coppices
Default: 30.
dist_max_cop=float
Maximum distance with other techniques for Coppices
Default: 600.
energy_tops_hf=float [required]
Energy for tops and branches in high forest in MWh/m³
Default: 0.49
energy_cormometric_vol_hf=float [required]
Energy for the whole tree in high forest (tops, branches and stem) in MWh/m³
Default: 1.97
energy_tops_cop=float [required]
Energy for tops and branches for Coppices in MWh/m³
Default: 0.55
