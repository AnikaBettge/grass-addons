NOME

r.green.biomassfor.theoretical.py - Estimates potential bioenergy depending on forest increment, forest management and forest treatment

PAROLE CHIAVE

SINOPSI

r.green.biomassfor.theoretical.py
r.green.biomassfor.theoretical.py --help
r.green.biomassfor.theoretical.py [energy_tops_hf=float] increment=name yield_surface=name management=name treatment=name output_prefix=name [energy_cormometric_vol_hf=float] [energy_tops_cop=float] [--overwrite] [--help] [--verbose] [--quiet] [--ui]

Flag:

--overwrite
Permetti al file di output di sovrascrivere file esistenti
--help
Print usage summary
--verbose
Output verboso del modulo
--quiet
Output quieto del modulo
--ui
Force launching GUI dialog

Parametri:

energy_tops_hf=float
Energy for tops and branches in high forest in MWh/m³
Default: 0.49
increment=name [required]
Map of increment
yield_surface=name [required]
Map of stand surface (ha)
management=name [required]
Map of forest management (1: high forest, 2:coppice)
treatment=name [required]
Map of forest treatment (1: final felling, 2:thinning)
output_prefix=name [required]
Prefix for potential bioenergy (HF,CC and total)
energy_cormometric_vol_hf=float
Energy for the whole tree in high forest (tops, branches and stem) in MWh/m³
Default: 1.97
energy_tops_cop=float
Energy for tops and branches for Coppices in MWh/m³
Default: 0.55
