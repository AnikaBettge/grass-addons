DESCRIPTION

v.civil.topo Create and modific Points-Breaklines maps, and triangulate, using triangle with v.triangle and nn with r.surf.nnbathy.

Topo

This section is under development and is still no usable.

Sup

This section follows the same philosophy as v.civil.road with the names of maps.

It has two option to triangulate the points and breaklines maps using triangle or nn. The output is a DEM and contour lines delimited by the input hull.

The others two options patch the output maps from triangle or nn with the input DEM map generating a new DEM map and contours lines map.
