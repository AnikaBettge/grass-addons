DESCRIPTION

v.in.osm imports OpenStreetMap data.

EXAMPLES

v.in.osm input="PG:host=localhost dbname=gis user=ostepok" table=planet_osm_line output=roads type=point,line where="highway is not null"

REQUIREMENTS

PostgreSQL, PostGIS, osm2pgsql
